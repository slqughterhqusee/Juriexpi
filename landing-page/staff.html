About Our Team

The Expeditions branch consists of engineers and designers who are utterly inspired by John Hammond's mision to recreate the Old World. Our goal is to present the park in a way that you, our loyal customer, can wholeheartedly enjoy without regret or a dull moment.
The Expeditions branch has been at work since around halfway through Jurassic Park's original production. We have countless ideas and drafts of your current spectacles and an overflowing pool of ideas to continue producing the best experience possible for you! If you see our Expeditions designers around the park, feel free to give them your love and appreciation for the wonderful place you stand in today!
