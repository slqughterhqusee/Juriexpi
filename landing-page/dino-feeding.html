Dino Feeding

Accessibility

If you use a mobility aid that is not suited for grassy terrain, it’s not recommended that you do the Dino Feeding.

Want to give your kids the experience of a lifetime? Come by to feed our baby Triceratops and Microceratus. And for parents—no worries! Your kids are safe. These silly little dinos aren’t very big, and are herbivores!


Hours and Pricing

Route

Participants and their chaperons should meet at the stand in the Pterodactyl Dome. Depending on how big each party is, our staff members will take you to the Microceratus or Triceratops exhibits. Our exhibit staff will section off an area for the dinosaurs to be fed. Each feeder will be given a handful of hay to feed the dinosaurs.
