Pterodactyl Dome

Accessibility

The tram and log ride both have accessible seating as transport. The Dome itself is spacious, and the food courts provide disability seating.

Are you a fan of aerial animals, or do you just want to relax while you grab a bite? Come see our Pterodactyls at the Pterodactyl Dome! Experience our flying friends without the separation of a fence!


Hours

Route and Shops

Route:
Located south of the Visitor Center. A tram will be provided or you may use the log ride as an alternative. The tram stops at the side road located in front of the Tyrannosaurus exhibit. Continue from the tram to the Pterodactyl Dome on foot following the side road eastward.
Shops:
Diners, gift shops, and food stands are all available during your experience in the Pterodactyl Dome!
