Caboose Cut-thru

Accessibility

Our caboose has disability-accessible seating and a ramp entry point.

T-Rexes and trains? There’s no better way to keep your kids happy. The train begins at 7:00AM, giving early Caboose Cut-thru buyers an exclusive truck tour before everyone else. Each ride lasts 25 minutes, with 15 minute intervals in between. This special ride allows you not only to have a family-fun experience, but also to view the T-Rex from above. You may even get to see it bring food back to its den!

Route

Take a special truck tour down to the T-Rex exhibit and board a train that cuts through the mountains of the T-Rex exhibit! After the train, you will be free to reenter and finish the Truck Tour.
