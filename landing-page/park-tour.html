Land Cruiser Tours

Accessibility

The Land Cruisers offer assistance with exit and entry, and can transport most mobility aids in the back trunk.

Each tour is 2-3 hours long with 15-20 minute intervals in between for cleaning. Tours are uniquely timed each day. For information on what times tours are taking place, check your itinerary with the available park times during your stay with us at Jurassic Park. The tour will be accompanied with dinosaur facts by Richard Kiley.


Hours and Pricing

Hours: 7:30 AM - 10:00 PM (CST) Check the itinerary here. Itineraries are only planned one month in advance due to weather prediction instability. Itineraries are subject to change.

Pricing Couples: $30.00 Singles: $20.00 Family: $35.00

Route

Tour begins at our Visitor Center and follows the main road. The tour also branches off of the main road onto the smaller dirt roads around the Triceratops and Velociraptor exhibits and back to the Visitor Center. Each tour follows a unique route!
